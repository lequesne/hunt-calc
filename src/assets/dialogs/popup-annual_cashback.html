Annual Owner Cashback

Buying into Huntlee and making smart choices for your energy supply entitles you to an annual cashback on your investment. Huntlee Plus will pay owners for the choices they make that contribute to the community's energy network. Earn money to help run the community energy network efficiently.

Choosing Smart Air, Smart Hot Water and an Electric Vehicle Charging Point will entitle you to $100 cashback every year for each choice. If you choose all three, your annual cashback will be $300 every year, year in year out.

Energy costs are dictated by peak demand – this is when the most people are using energy at the same time. By working with Huntlee Plus to smooth out the peaks in energy demand, you will be saving yourself and the community huge expense. The annual cashback is a return on the contribution you are making to Huntlee's community energy network.

Product details

Community Energy Network – Every household will receive free solar panels on the roof of their home. This will instantly help generate power for the community and provide access to a lower energy tariff for residents.

EarthSource – The return in energy bill savings for EarthSource are so great that this product does not attract an annual cashback. In addition to a massively efficient system that delivers air conditioning, heating and hot water, the environmental benefits of this choice are compelling. If Earth Source is selected, Smart Air and Smart Water are not required.

Smart Air – we will pay you $100 every year to let us manage the speed of your air conditioner especially on hot days. You will still manage the temperature and if you want, we can even pre-cool your home so it is comfortable for you when you come home from work or ahead of the day's heat. We do this by installing a remote controller on your air conditioning unit, which gives us permission to adjust the speed of your air conditioner under conditions we've agreed with you.

Did you know?

Studies have found that there is no effect on people's comfort from reducing the speed of an air conditioner by 25 percent. (Ausgrid Demand Management CoolSaver Interim Report, 2015)

http://www.ausgrid.com.au/coolsaver

There are no upfront infrastructure costs when you choose this product.

Smart Hot Water – again, we will pay you $100 every year, to heat your water in off peak periods or when our community energy network is producing more energy than is needed and it can be used to heat your water, ready for when you want to use it. We guarantee you will always have the hot water you want when you need it. Only electric hot water units are eligible for this offer.
There are no upfront infrastructure costs when you choose this product.

Electric Vehicle Charging Point – electric vehicles are the way of the future. Iron out your petrol bills as well as your energy bills by moving ahead of the curve and installing an electric vehicle charging point. By choosing this product, we will pay you $100 every year to automatically power up your electric vehicle when it is cheapest for you, for example when you park it in your garage overnight. In return, the community benefits as this product will help support the efficiency of the energy network.
There are no upfront infrastructure costs when you choose this product.
